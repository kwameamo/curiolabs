Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing. If you don't see your question here, please contact us.

How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex sites like e-commerce platforms can take 6-8 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance for websites?
Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, backup management, and technical support. You can choose monthly or annual plans based on your needs.
Can you help with existing technical problems?
Absolutely! We specialize in troubleshooting and resolving existing technical issues. Whether you're dealing with hardware problems, software conflicts, network issues, or performance concerns, our team can diagnose and fix them. We offer both remote and on-site support depending on the nature of the problem.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations. We believe in supporting organizations that make a positive impact, and we're happy to discuss how we can accommodate your budget while providing the services you need. Please contact us for more information about our non-profit rates.
What areas do you serve?
For digital services like website development and technical writing, we serve clients worldwide. For in-person services such as IT support, hardware repairs, and network installations, we primarily serve the local area and surrounding communities. However, we also offer remote IT support for clients outside our immediate service area.
